# App Store Age Rating Answers

Prepared 21 July 2026. Apple introduced additional social-capability questions in July 2026, so use the labels visible in App Store Connect and map them to the positions below.

## In-app controls and capabilities

| Capability | Answer | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Parental controls | No | AT CAPACITY does not provide parent-managed controls. |
| Age assurance | No | Accounts are coach-created, but there is no formal age-verification service. |
| User-generated content | Yes | Clients submit DMs, check-ins, documents and photos. |
| Messaging and chat | Yes | Private one-to-one messaging exists between client and coach. |
| Social media or public social features | No | There are no public profiles, feeds, follower systems or public chat rooms. |
| Unrestricted web access | No | The app is limited to AT CAPACITY routes and opens approved external links in the system browser. |
| Advertising | No | The native client app has no advertising. |
| In-app purchases | No | Enrolment and payment happen outside the app. |

## Content frequency

- Violence, weapons, horror, sexual content, nudity, alcohol, tobacco, drugs, gambling, contests and loot boxes: **None**
- Profanity or crude humour: **Infrequent** because private user-authored messages can contain free-form language; AT CAPACITY does not publish editorial profanity.
- Medical or treatment information: **Infrequent** because the app records injuries/health context and may advise a client to seek a qualified professional, while explicitly avoiding diagnosis and treatment.
- Wellness topics: select the available fitness/wellness option where Apple presents one; do not classify AT CAPACITY as medical treatment.

## Category and override

- Made for Kids: **No**
- Age category: **Override to 16+**
- Reason: AT CAPACITY's privacy policy and intended-client policy exclude children under 16, and the app handles private health, body, injury and optional cycle information.
- Age Suitability URL: `https://gordy-elliott-site.vercel.app/privacy`

If Apple's calculated rating is already 16+ or higher, retain the calculated result rather than applying a lower override.
